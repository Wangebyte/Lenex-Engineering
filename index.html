Who We Are

Trusted Consultants. Proven Results.

Lenex Engineering Consultants is a multidisciplinary engineering and consulting firm headquartered in Thika along Garissa Road, Kenya. We bring together experienced engineers, technicians and project managers united by one goal — delivering innovative, reliable, and cost-effective solutions.

From food-processing machinery and boiler systems to CCTV, solar energy, and water infrastructure, we are the single partner our clients trust for the full lifecycle of an engineering project: consultation, design, installation, and maintenance.
